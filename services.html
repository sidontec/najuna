Our Services

Najuna General Supplies, we pride ourselves on delivering a diverse range of services designed to meet the dynamic needs of our clients. Our offerings include:

Fumigation and Fire Safety Solutions

We offer professional fumigation services to ensure pest-free environments, along with the installation and maintenance of fire fighting equipment, including fire extinguishers, to enhance safety and compliance in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

Printing Services

We provide high-quality printing solutions for all your needs, including promotional materials like brochures, flyers, and banners, as well as custom stationery and branded items. Our services ensure precision, creativity, and timely delivery to make your ideas stand out.

Supply of Uniforms

Supply of Stationery and Office Equipment

We provide a wide range of stationery and office supplies, including paper, pens, folders, and other essential items, along with office equipment such as printers, copiers, and computers to keep your workspace fully equipped and efficient.

Cleaning Services and Garden Maintenance

Fumigation services

NGS is registered with the Tropical Pesticides Research Institute (TPRI) and holds a permit for fumigation and pest control services. With over 10 years of experience, our trained fumigators offer both chemical and non-chemical treatments to effectively manage pests and rodents, ensuring a safe and pest-free environment.

Transport and Logistics services

We offer comprehensive logistics solutions for all cargo types, from consolidated shipments to full container transport, including full and partial charters. With access to a private fleet and a network of trusted third-party carriers, we provide reliable transport services across Tanzania and the East African region. Our cross-border road freight services ensure safe and secure delivery of cargo from Tanzania to all countries in East Africa. Additionally, our experienced team works closely with your business to integrate seamless freight management into your daily operations.

Security Systems

We specialize in delivering reliable security solutions across Tanzania. Our expert team and advanced technology ensure the safety of residential, commercial, and industrial properties with services like CCTV surveillance, access control systems, and alarm solutions. We prioritize your security by offering customizable systems that integrate seamlessly into your existing setup. With 24/7 monitoring and proactive solutions, we are committed to safeguarding what matters most to you with efficiency and reliability.
