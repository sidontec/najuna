Welcome to Najuna General Supplies Company Ltd

A distinguished Tanzanian enterprise established on April 19, 2010. Registered under company number 75959 and accredited by Tanzania's National Procurement System (NeST), we are committed to providing exceptional procurement, supply, and related services across diverse sectors.

  • Trusted Tanzanian Enterprise: Established in 2010 and accredited by the National Procurement System (NeST), delivering reliable and high-quality procurement and supply services.
  • Comprehensive Offerings: Providing building materials, office equipment, fumigation, fire safety solutions, printing services, and machinery maintenance, supported by a skilled professional team.
  • Commitment to Excellence: Focused on customer satisfaction, ethical practices, and innovative solutions tailored to meet diverse client needs.
